Assassin's Creed Female Characters

Assassin's Creed is an open-world, action-adventure, and stealth game franchise published by Ubisoft and developed mainly by its studio Ubisoft Montreal using the game engine Anvil and its more advanced derivatives.

Elise

Elise is determined to secure her place in the Templar dynasty amidst the chaos of the French Revolution. Her quest leads her to cross paths with Arno and establish an unlikely bond with him’, adds the blurb.

Aveline

Aveline de Grandpré is Assassin's Creed 3: Liberation's brave and virtuous protagonist. This stunning game is set in 18th-century New Orleans and takes its players on an epic adventure filled with great characters, dark secrets, as well as some of the best-hidden blades in the franchise.

Evie Frye

Evie Frye is another iconic female Assassin's Creed character who was inspired by a real-life gang member called Alice Diamond who was a member of The Forty Elephants Gang. Assassin's Creed Syndicate's most beloved female assassin helped the British Brotherhood fight many of their evil foes.
